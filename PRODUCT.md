# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary user is the owner/operator's brother, working through restaurant campaign leads and WhatsApp outreach. He needs the app to feel simple, clear, and dependable whether he is sitting at a desktop, checking a tablet, or working from a phone.

## Product Purpose

The app helps an operator import leads, configure a campaign, build a WhatsApp message, send or queue outreach, and track what is sent or pending. Success means the operator can complete campaign work quickly without fighting layout, tiny controls, or desktop-only flows.

## Brand Personality

Nice, easy, focused, and premium without becoming complicated. The interface should feel like a calm operator tool: direct, readable, and confident.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboard patterns, cluttered bulk-sender tools, tiny desktop-only controls, overly dark hacker-style visuals, and mobile layouts that hide core campaign actions.

## Design Principles

Make the primary workflow obvious on every screen size.

Keep campaign setup, message preview, and lead actions reachable without visual noise.

Use responsive layouts that rethink the workflow instead of shrinking the desktop screen.

Prioritize readable text, large touch targets, and clear status feedback.

Preserve a premium visual tone while keeping the interface practical for repeated daily use.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable type sizes, visible focus states, touch targets of at least 44px, sufficient contrast, and reduced-motion-friendly interaction patterns. The app should remain usable with keyboard navigation and on narrow mobile screens.
